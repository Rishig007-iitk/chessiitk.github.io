About Us

CHESS CLUB IIT KANPUR

We at Chess Club IIT Kanpur conduct various tournaments and sessions for the people at our campus and other colleges. Our major initiatives include Chess in Slums, where we visit remote areas and introduce the beautiful game to underprivileged children, and a FIDE-styled IITK Chess Championship Cycle comprising of events like Grand Swiss, Grand Prix, a Candidates Tournament and an IITK Chess Championship Match. Also, we are one of the only few institutes to incorporate such a comprehensive chess structure for the campus community.

Championship Cycle

♟️ Championship Qualification System

Our championship follows a structured and competitive format designed to identify the strongest challenger to face the reigning champion.

👑

Current Champion

The current reigning champion is Spandan Pati, who holds the title and awaits the next official challenger.

🏆 Selection Process

  • 3 players qualify from the Grand Swiss
  • 3 players qualify from the Chess Cup
  • 1 player runner-up of previous championship
  • 1 player best performer from remaining pool

Total: 8 elite players

🔁 Final Qualifier

The 8 players compete in a double round-robin tournament. The player with the best overall performance becomes the official challenger.

⚔️ Championship Match

The challenger competes against the defending champion. The winner becomes the new champion.

🗳️ Interim Leadership

In pending situations, a temporary champion is selected through a voting process involving players, coordinators, and the captain.
